Card2

Statement

There are $N$ cards. Each card is numbered from 1 to $N$ in order, with the 1st card on top and the $N$th card at the bottom.

The following actions are repeated until only one card remains: Firstly, the top card is discarded. Then, the new top card is moved to the bottom of the stack.

For example, consider $N = 4$. The cards are initially arranged in the order 1234 from top to bottom. Discarding 1 leaves 234. Moving 2 to the bottom results in 342. Discarding 3 leaves 42, and moving 4 to the bottom results in 24. Finally, discarding 2 leaves card number 4.

Given $N$, write a program to find out which card remains at the end.

Input

The first line contains the integer $N$ ($1 \le N \le 500\,000$).

Output

Print the number of the card that remains at the end.
