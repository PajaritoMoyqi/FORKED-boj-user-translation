Numbers Less Than X

Statement

Given a sequence $A$ consisting of $N$ integers and an integer $X$, write a program that prints out all the numbers in $A$ that are less than $X$.

Input

The first line provides $N$ and $X$ ($1 \le N, X \le 10\,000$).

The second line provides $N$ integers that make up the sequence $A$. All given integers are greater than or equal to 1, and less than or equal to 10,000.

Output

Print out the numbers less than $X$ in the order they were received, separated by a space. There is at least one number less than $X$.
