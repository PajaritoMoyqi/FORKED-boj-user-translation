Cat League

문제

In the Cat League, teams consisting of cats compete against each other.

The International Cat Sports Federation has specified the conditions that a team must satisfy:

  • The team must have $m$ players.
  • Each player's price is an integer ranging from $1$ to $n-1$ (inclusive).
  • The prices of any two different players must be distinct.
  • The least common multiple of the prices of any two players is $n$.

A wealthy cat conglomerate has recently taken an interest in the Cat League and decided to establish a new team.

They entrusted the team management to Yeongchul, who is in need of quick funds. Yeongchul plans to assemble the team with minimal expenses and pocket the rest.

Help Yeongchul determine a team that meets the approval criteria while minimizing the sum of the players' prices, ensuring the team is approved!

입력

Two integers, $n$ and $m$, are given separated by a space. ($2 \leq m \leq n \leq 10^6$)

출력

If there exists a team composition that meets the approval criteria, output $m$ distinct integers separated by spaces in any order. These values represent the player prices in any one team that is approved, and the sum of these prices is minimized.

If there is no team composition that meets the approval criteria, output -1.

There is no way to form a team that satisfies the approval criteria of the International Cat Sports Federation.

Yeongchul is going to be fired... :(

The team composed of players with player prices $33$, $6$, and $22$ satisfies the following criteria:

  • The team has $m (=3)$ players.
  • The player prices $33$, $6$, and $22$ are all less than or equal to $n-1 (=65)$.
  • $33$, $6$, and $22$ are distinct.
  • The least common multiple of any pair of player prices matches $n (=66)$.
    • The least common multiple of $33$ and $6$ is $66$.
    • The least common multiple of $33$ and $22$ is $66$.
    • The least common multiple of $6$ and $22$ is $66$.

Therefore, it is possible to obtain approval from the International Cat Sports Federation.

Additionally, there is no other team composition with a lower sum of player prices that satisfies the approval criteria of the International Cat Sports Federation.

Behold Yeongchul, proudly with the meticulously crafted ultimate dream team.
