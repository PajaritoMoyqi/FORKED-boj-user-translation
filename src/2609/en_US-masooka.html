Greatest Common Divisor and Least Common Multiple

Statement

Write a program that takes two natural numbers as input and outputs their greatest common divisor (GCD) and least common multiple (LCM).

Input

The first line contains two natural numbers. These numbers are natural numbers less than or equal to 10,000, and they are separated by a space.

Output

On the first line, output the greatest common divisor of the two numbers given as input. On the second line, output their least common multiple.
