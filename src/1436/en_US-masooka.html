Director Schom

Statement

It is said that the number 666 represents the apocalypse. Therefore, many blockbuster movies often use titles containing 666. Director Schom, the creator of the End of the World series, decides to name his movies differently to surpass directors like George Lucas and Peter Jackson.

An apocalyptic number is a number that contains at least three consecutive 6s. The smallest apocalyptic number is 666, followed by 1666, 2666, 3666, and so on. Accordingly, Schom names his first movie End of the World 666, the second movie End of the World 1666, and so forth. In general, the title of the $N$th movie in the series is End of the World followed by the $N$th smallest apocalyptic number.

Write a program to output the number in the title of Schom's $N$th movie. Note that Schom always produces these movies in order and does not make any other movies.

Input

The first line contains $N$. $N$ is a natural number less than or equal to 10,000.

Output

Output the number that appears in the title of the $N$th movie.
