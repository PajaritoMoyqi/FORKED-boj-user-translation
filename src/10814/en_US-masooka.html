Age-based Sorting

Statement

Write a program that sorts the members of an online judge by age in increasing order. If members have the same age, they should be sorted in the order they joined.

Input

The first line contains the number of online judge members, $N$ ($1 \le N \le 100\,000$).

From the second line to the next $N$ lines, the age and name of each member are given, separated by a space. The age is an integer greater than or equal to 1 and less than or equal to 200, and the name is a string consisting of upper and lower case alphabet letters, with a length of no more than 100 characters. The input is given in the order of joining.

Output

Print the age and name of each online judge member on a separate line, in increasing order of age and, for members with the same age, in the order they joined. There should be a total of $N$ lines.
